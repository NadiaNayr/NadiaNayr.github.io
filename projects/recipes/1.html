Lemon Pasta

This is a recipe

Ingredient
Lemon juice
Parmasean cheese
butter

When I make this I don't use a cetain amount, I change the amounts depending on how i'm feeling.

Making the noodles:

  1. Fill a pot halfway with water
  2. Put the pot on a stove and let the water boil
  3. Grab a box of uncooked spaghetti noodles and put it in the pot
  4. Wait 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Strain the water out of the noodles

Making the sauce:

  1. put butter, parmasean cheese, and lemon juice in a pot/pan
  2. Put on stove on low/medium heat
  3. stir for almost the whole time
  4. Once the butter melts, turn off the heat and add the noodles to pot/pan
  5. stir for about a minute, then put noodles and sauce into a bowl
  6. Usually, I put some of the lemon juice straight onto the noodles, but this step is optional
