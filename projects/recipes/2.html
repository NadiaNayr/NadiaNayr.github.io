Homemade Lemonade - 15 min

I chose this simple lemonade because it is not overly sweet but is still not bitter! My friends said, "I always make this recipe, it tastes so good!"

Ingredients
4 Lemons
2/3 cup of sugar
Water
  • This makes enough lemonade for a pitcher (2 quarts)
  1. juice 4 lemons
  2. add the 2/3 cup of sugar and mix
  3. fill the rest of the pitcher with water
  4. Mix again
  • adding more water/sugar makes it less bitter
  • More lemon will make it more bitter
