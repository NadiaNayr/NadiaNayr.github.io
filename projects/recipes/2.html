Homemade Lemonade

This simple lemonade is not overly sweet but is still not bitter!

Ingredients
4 Lemons
2/3 cup of sugar
Water
  • This makes enough lemonade for a pitcher (2 quarts)
  1. juice 4 lemons
  2. add the 2/3 cup of sugar and mix
  3. fill the rest of the pitcher with water
  4. Mix again
