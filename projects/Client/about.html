About Us

Welcome to For the Historian!

Step into a treasure trove of military history right in the heart of Gettysburg, PA! Since 2013, we've been proud to serve as your destination for all things military history—from the pivotal battles of the Civil War to the conflicts that have shaped our modern world.

Whether you're a battlefield visitor, a dedicated history buff, or a curious explorer, you'll find something special here. Our shelves are packed with an extensive collection of new and used books, including rare titles you won't find anywhere else. But we're more than just books! Browse our carefully curated selection of historical miniatures, collectibles, and magazines that bring history to life.

Our knowledgeable staff is passionate about what we do, and we're always here to help you find exactly what you're looking for—or discover something new. Looking for that perfect figurine to complete your collection? We've got you covered. Need a recommendation for your next deep dive into history? We're ready with suggestions.

We love connecting with our community through author signings and special events, and our convenient webstore means you can shop with us anytime, anywhere. Plus, with parking available for customers, visiting us is always easy.

Come see why history enthusiasts call us their favorite spot in Gettysburg. We can't wait to share our passion for history with you!
